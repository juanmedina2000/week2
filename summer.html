how to pick ripe watermelon:

Watermelon should be firm and symmetrical, without bruises, cuts, or dents.
It should feel heavy for its size and have a creamy yellow spot where the melon sat on the ground to ripen in the sun.
Precut melon flesh should be dense, firm, and appear moist.
